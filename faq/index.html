What is SyncSwap?

SyncSwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built natively on zkSync Era, enabling users to swap tokens with low fees, zero slippage, and full custody of their assets.

Which digital assets are supported on SyncSwap?

SyncSwap supports a wide variety of tokens including major assets such as BTC, ETH, SOL, AVAX, and LINK, which can be traded instantly inside layer-2 pools.

Is SyncSwap audited and secure?

Yes, SyncSwap smart contracts are audited by top-tier Web3 security firms. The protocol uses non-custodial smart contracts, meaning users retain full ownership of their credentials.

How does SyncSwap reduce transaction gas fees?

SyncSwap utilizes zk-rollup batching technology on zkSync Era. This technology bundles transactions together to significantly decrease the gas fees compared to traditional Ethereum trades.
